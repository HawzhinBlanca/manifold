# Credits & Third-Party Assets

## Deep-space sky backdrop
- **NASA's Scientific Visualization Studio — Deep Star Maps 2020** (`Content/Sky/T_StarMap`, authored
  from `Tools/Art/assets/starmap_milkyway_2k.jpg`). An equirectangular all-sky panorama plotted from
  the Hipparcos-2, Tycho-2, and Gaia DR2 star catalogs. **Public domain** (NASA / U.S. Government work).
  Source: https://svs.gsfc.nasa.gov/4851/ — downsized and tone-mapped for use as the game's HDRI sky.

Everything else in MANIFOLD (kernels, correspondence engine, gameplay, procedural materials, audio,
meshes) is original work authored for this project.
